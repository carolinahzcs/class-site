He reaches his hand out, wanting to touch the life in front of him, but he just touches air. It's so close, he thinks he can grab it, but eyes lie. He's running, thinking he can get a grasp if he reaches far enough but nothing. It's frustration that starts to settle, he wonders why it's so unattainable when it's right in front of him. He thinks, "why is it there if I can't do anything about it?" He sees that if he holds his hand far enough and shuts an eye that it appears he's gotten a hold of it. But that is nothing but a lie too.

this tranquil being

the fish practices wisdom

to awake the world
